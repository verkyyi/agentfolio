# Software Engineer, Enterprise Data Platform — Notion

San Francisco, CA | $230,000–$300,000

We're looking for a Software Engineer to help design and build Notion's core enterprise data platform, including our data lakehouse, critical data pipelines, and security integrations. You'll own and operate large-scale data infrastructure powering AI, analytics, and search, with a focus on reliability, cost optimization, and compliance for enterprise customers.

## What You'll Do
- Design, build, and operate Notion's data lakehouse and core data pipelines at scale
- Build and maintain data infrastructure powering AI features, analytics, and search
- Improve reliability, cost efficiency, and performance of data systems
- Implement data security and compliance features for enterprise customers (e.g., EKM)
- Collaborate with product, AI, and infrastructure teams to deliver data capabilities

## Requirements
- 5+ years of software engineering experience, with a focus on data infrastructure
- Strong proficiency in Python, Java, or Scala
- Experience building and operating large-scale data pipelines (Spark, Flink, or Kafka)
- Solid SQL skills and experience with data modeling
- Experience with cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Familiarity with data lakehouse architectures (Delta Lake, Iceberg, or Hudi)

## Nice to Have
- Experience with Kubernetes and containerized workloads
- Background in data security, encryption, or compliance (SOC 2, FedRAMP)
- Contributions to open-source data tools
- Experience building platforms used by internal teams
